Hurry Up!

“That is my problem with life, I just rush through it, like I’m being chased.”

— Miranda July, The Man on the Stairs

  The Man on the Stairs is a story about a hasty woman reevaluating her life under the threat of impending murder. She notices how slowly and meticulously the man approaches her room, and considers all of her own personal failures under the ensuing stress. She realizes how her own haste has failed her. She never truly enjoyed her experiences since she was always rushing to the next thing. She never cared for her friends because she was waiting for better friends to come along. She realized that her haste was ruining her life and, should she live pledged to live her life slowly. She then confronts the killer and tells him to leave her house.

TL;DR: Give life time to happen.
